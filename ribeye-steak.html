Ribeye Steak


Image by Paras Kapoor

Ribeye steak is a cut of beef that is known for its rich, juicy, and flavorful meat. It is usually grilled or pan-seared. Served as a main course with various sides such as vegetables, potatoes, or salad.

Ingredients

  • 2 Ribeye Steaks (about 1 lb each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Take the steaks out of the refrigerator and let them sit at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  3. Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat.
  4. Rub the steaks with olive oil, garlic, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  5. Place the steaks in the skillet and sear for 2-3 minutes on each side.
  6. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and cook for 6-8 minutes for medium-rare doneness, or longer for medium or well-done steaks.
  7. Remove the skillet from the oven and let the steaks rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
